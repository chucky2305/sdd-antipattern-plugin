## AP-06: Not defining the stage (MVP vs v1)

**What it means:** An MVP spec and a v1 spec are completely different documents with different tradeoffs. Conflating them leads to over-engineering early or under-delivering later.

**What a good spec includes:**
- An explicit stage declaration: "This is an MVP: we're validating [specific assumption], rough edges are OK" OR "This is v1: quality and completeness matter"
- Everything else in the spec flows from this declaration
