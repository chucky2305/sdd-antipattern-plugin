## AP-03: Not defining your target audience

**What it means:** Every design and implementation decision is implicitly about who's using the software. Without a defined audience, the team (and the AI) builds for a person who doesn't exist.

**What a good spec includes:**
- A one-paragraph description of the primary user: their context, technical level, and what they're trying to accomplish
- If multiple user types exist: they are ranked by priority, not treated as equal
